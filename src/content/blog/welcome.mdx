# Welcome to Arraz!
### 2026-03-25

"Arraz", slang term for a dart, is a passion project that I made during one of my leaves from military service. 
I was playing darts with my dad and found most of the scorekeeping websites I tried were 
either broken, bloated, outdated or for some other reason didn't work to my satisfaction.

Over a week I created Arraz, and implemented the first and my most played game, X01. This project
quickly took up a lot of my time, and soon enough Killer, Cricket and Shanghai were added too. 
I'm still working on adding more games - So if you can't find your favorite, contact me and I'll make
sure it's added eventually!

The goal of Arraz was to be a minimal scorekeeping website with no login or other distracting features. While I've been
researching darts for this project, I've learned there are a LOT of interesting darts history, cool games, regional variants,
and passionate players. I hope this tool will at least be of use to some people!

As for features, the primary feature is of course the scorekeeping pages. Some games, like X01 where you play with all beds, it features
an interactive dartboard where you click the bed you hit. Others, where most beds are irrelevent, e.g Shanghai or Cricket, there's 
a simpler layout. This has been a really fun part of this project; trying to come up with the best UI for all the different games as they
all play out in different ways. I can't say I'm *quite* content with all of them yet, but I'll keep on making slight modifications until it plays
as smoothly as possible.

After the first games were created, I also created rule pages for all the games, so Arraz can be a place to discover new games as well. I also
added a "Guides" section, with a board setup guide with dimensions and a printable X01 checkout chart. I hope this can make Arraz be a place with content
for both new players and well-seasoned ones. 

Thank you for reading, and don't hesitate with reaching out with suggestions or feedback! You can find my contact info under "contact" in the footer of the front page.
