# Future of Arraz
### 2026-03-27

What's coming next for Arraz? In this post I'll go over what my plans are for Arraz and some challanges tied to it.

## Games
Adding more games will likely be a priority for a while. I've made a list of games I want to add. 
Cricket, X01, Killer and Shanghai are available as of writing. If you've got other suggestions, feel free to contact me!

- Around the clock
- Gotcha
- Bob's 27
- Splitscore/Halve-it/Bermuda Triangle

I'd also like to make more variations of the most popular ones, as rules/playstyles may vary across regions.
Specifically for Cricket, while I've not played these myself, I'd love to add Camdem Throwdown, Scram, Bowlers and Batters 
and Tactics. 


## Features

The UX philosophy for Arraz has been keeping it minimal, reduce bloat and keep friction low. 
It should be simple to quickly spin up a game, choose your config and start playing. However, at the same time,
I want Arraz to have more complex features that allow more experienced players to improve their game.

#### Profiles

I believe adding profiles may be crucial for experienced players to track their progress and see where they have
the most room for improvement. A profile infrastructure would also allow me to develop more neat features, like a 
game history. It's going to require some planning and thought to make accounts as unobtrusive and undistracting as possible,
and only be necessary for the particularly interested, as to not increase friction for players who do not need these 
features.

#### X01 Checkouts

On the X01 scorekeeping page, I'd love to show a list of suggested checkouts once you're in the checkout range. I do already 
have a checkout chart available for printing, but a dedicated checkout suggestion based on your current score could
give you multiple different options and you would not need to look for your score on a poster.

#### End-of-game Stats

I'd love to add more stats on the game-over modals that pop up, and honestly just rework those overall to make them significantly more substantial.
Specifically stuff like hit% for Shanghai, Cricket, Killer etc and Points per dart (which is already on screen throughout the game) for X01. There's 
a lot that can be displayed, like total damage in Killer,or a heat map in X01.
